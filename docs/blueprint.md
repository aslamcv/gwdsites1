# **App Name**: WellBill

## Core Features:

- Customer Management: Add, update, and manage customer information, including contact details and billing addresses.
- Drilling Cost Calculation: Dynamically calculate the cost of drilling based on depth, materials used, labor costs, and other relevant factors using a generative AI tool.
- Invoice Generation: Generate professional invoices with detailed billing information, including line items for materials, labor, and taxes.
- Payment Tracking: Record and track payments made by customers, with options to mark invoices as paid or partially paid.
- Reporting: Generate reports on revenue, outstanding invoices, and other key financial metrics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep sky blue (#00BFFF), reminiscent of clean water and precision.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E0FFFF), a lighter tint of the primary, which will support readability.
- Accent color: Turquoise (#40E0D0), analogous to the primary hue, will distinguish certain buttons and links.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif font will make for easy readability and a modern feel.
- Use simple, line-based icons to represent actions and data, aligning with a clean and professional look.
- Design a clear and intuitive layout with well-organized sections for easy navigation.
- Incorporate subtle animations, like fade-ins, on new information loading into the UI.
